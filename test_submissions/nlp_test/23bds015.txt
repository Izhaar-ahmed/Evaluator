Question:
What does TF-IDF stand for in NLP, and what is its primary purpose?
Answer:
TF-IDF stands for Token Frequency - Inverse Data Format. It is used in NLP to format raw text tokens into a structured data format suitable for machine learning pipelines. The Token Frequency part counts how often a token appears, while Inverse Data Format helps convert those tokens into numeric arrays.
